The Shape Dashboard was developed by eHealthConnecticut in conjunction with the Connecticut Department of Public Health as part of the SHAPE grant (State Public Health Actions to Prevent and Control Diabetes, Heart Disease, Obesity and Associated Risk Factors and Promote School Health-1305), through funding from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.

The goal of the SHAPE project is to leverage healthcare providers' health information technology to develop processes to improve hypertension and diabetes awareness and management among their patient populations.

Through this quality measure dashboard, healthcare providers can collect and report aggregate data for NQF 0018: Controlling High Blood Pressure, and NQF 0059: Diabetes: Hemoglobin A1c Poor Control.
